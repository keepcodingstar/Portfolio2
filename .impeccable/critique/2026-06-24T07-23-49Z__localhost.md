## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**Distinctive and now consistent.** The second-order slop risk from the last run — the work zone reading as the saturated *editorial-typographic* lane — is resolved: it's now an instrument readout (gauge tick-scale, viewfinder brackets, HUD markers) that only this site would make. The about section is a vertical instrument rail rather than four identical glass cards. The galleries read as deliberate cosmos portholes / a product contact-sheet instead of empty placeholders. The repeated per-section eyebrow was trimmed (kept on Space/Creative + Ground; dropped on Work/About/UIGallery).

**Deterministic scan:** home zone components return 16 findings, **all advisory** (the gallery color-field gradient tints) — **zero real defects**. The one prior warning (the `side-tab` ban) was fixed in Phase 0.

## What's Working
1. **The instrument motif is now a thread, not a hint** — side-nav altimeter, work-zone gauges, the about rail spine, and the count-up readouts all speak one language. Distinctiveness compounds.
2. **The journey is uniformly strong** — the weak between-zones (galleries, about) were lifted to the level of Space/Sky; no more quality cliff after the top half.
3. **Motion language is consistent** — the between-zones now reveal on scroll like the hero zones (reduced-motion + no-JS safe, gated on `body.altitude-ready`).
